What are the
Frequently Asked Questions?

Who does YSI consist of ?

The YSI Global team consists of 6 people in Oslo. We have regional YSI networks around the globe,
called YSI Locals: YSI Bangladesh, YSI SEA (Southeast Asia) and YSI Shanghai. Nearly all of us are in our early twenties and doing our best to make the world a better place.

So is YSI the same as Earthpreneurs ?

No, YSI runs an innovation program which one has to apply to set up, while Earthpreneurs is an open community which anyone can join or form local communities to create teams to solve SDGs locally.

How can we make an impact with Earthpreneurs ?

This is largely up to you to define, yet I have some quick keywords right here: Sharing – Matching – Testing – Innovating-Creating – Distributing

How many members should the local community have? Are there any limitations ?

You have freedom to make decisions and grow your own community. How you grow it and the limit is up to you. The sheer variety of ways you do it is proof of innovation and this way we can learn from each other.
No matter how small or large you are, remember to follow these guidelines.

Can I use trademarked names in my items?

When deciding whether or not to use a trademarked name as a title or tag you should ask yourself this question; "Am I describing what it is or what it the item is like?"
Using trademarked names to describe what your file actually is is acceptable. Using them to describe what your file is like is not.

Is the local community bound to act under the support from the Head community ?

No! We are making some activities that we can share across areas that we would like you to join however you are free to choose yourself. I am more of a facilitator rather than any form of superior.
You also know your local area and what is needed right now far more than I ever will, because I am not at your location. It might be better to get some fresh perspective on issues you are struggling with however.

Are we supposed to initiate our own organisations or social ventures? Or we'll be encouraging the community members to start their businesses and initiatives (supporting SDGs) ?

Again, you can choose. You can do everything from making a group and small events to creating a learning platform in the local language to finance your activities while building a community. It depends on your own approach and interests.

If I belong to a startup company, is it possible to create the community in cooperation with that startup or I should create this individually ?

Yes it’s possible. In our experience if you build it with a community and a local team we have seen that you will experience a more rapid growth than otherwise.

What do members need to be active members of your community ?

They need to be interested in solving the sustainable development goals or learn more about them. We also expect our members to be tolerant and respectful for diversity while having an inquisitive attitude to life. Our members want to save the world.
For peer to peer learning we need to build a set of skills relevant to startups, leadership and inspirations. Also in regards to building good teams since our members will need to build their own communities. Community leadership is also a relevant skill that could be useful to develop within the organisation. They need to feel belonging and meaning, also confidence to take extraordinary action and bring to life the change needed to reach the sustainable development goals.

Can YSI Global help the local community to connect with possible mentors and trainers to work with us online for specific limited time ?

Yes, there’s absolutely no issue.

Can we raise funds ?

Yes. (Personal fundraising however is not advised)

Is it possible for YSI Global to join us in dealing with such bodies/donors/agencies ?

Yes, of course. We can also join through video!

After the community expands and engages a lot of people, can that local chapter become a non-profit or a NGO ?

Definitely.

How can I create a YSI Local chapter in my country or area ?

We will open applications during 2018. When this happens we will notify all groups of Earthpreneurs about this process.

If the person gets selected at the YSI innovative program, will it then become possible to create YSI in his/her local community? Or are the applications are separated ?

The applications are seperate. You do not need to have attended YSI Globals Innovation Program to apply to set it up.

Earthpreneurs X is hosting an event in collaboration with Y in my country physically. We would like to know if YSI can supporting us financially to make this happens. If yes, how would we proceed ?

I am sad to say that we do not have the capacity or funds to support any such activities financially, however we will do our best to support with anything else we can muster in terms of human resources, network and otherwise. Especially when it comes to finding good business models or partnerships. (example: Earthpreneurs Myanmar contacting regarding an event they are having and get a recommendation of speakers for a live stream).
