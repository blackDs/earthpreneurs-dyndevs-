Who are EARTHPRENEURS
Which type of movement are we?

An earthpreneur is a person who intend to make sustainability business as usual. First by addressing the 17 Sustainable Development Goals issued by the United Nations. The term was coined by the organization YSI (Young Sustainable Impact) in 2016 and has later developed into a global movement lead by youth around the world.

Mission: Empowering youth to solve sustainability challenges by creating measurable impact
Vision: A thriving sustainable world

We are a movement to alter business , we are attempting to facilitate and develop a global community for those who wish to address the 17 Sustainable Development goals through new business. Both in new companies and within big corporations.

Earthpreneurs goal is to help create a sustainable world by facilitating partnerships, friendships, and opportunities for youth.

We believe innovation and entrepreneurship can solve most of our challenges in the world,

that youth is the driving force, and in collaboration with the experienced and established,
it can be done.
